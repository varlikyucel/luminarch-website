About The LuminArch

LuminArch is a single-player architectural design game where creativity meets science. Players design simple structures using walls, windows, and sunshades—then see how their choices affect sunlight, glare, and shadow in real-time. Once the design is complete, our simulation system scores their building's daylight performance using real-world sun data.

Built for architecture students and enthusiasts, LuminArch turns daylight design into an engaging and educational challenge.

LuminArch is a serious game designed and developed by Varlık Yücel as part of his PhD thesis, under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Dr. Sema Alaçam at Istanbul Technical University's Architectural Design Computing Program.
